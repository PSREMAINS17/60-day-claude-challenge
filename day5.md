MAJOR LEARNINGS:
The broader lesson: without context, a model optimises for completeness — covering everything a beginner might need. With context, it can optimise for fit — covering what this beginner needs, skipping what they don't, and matching the format to how they actually learn.
The prompt didn't get longer — it got more specific. That's what drove the quality gap.
